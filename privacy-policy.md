# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 26, 2026

WanderBloom ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use the WanderBloom iOS app.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Account registration details (name, email address)
- Travel preferences and destination wishlists
- Trip notes, itineraries, and journal entries you create

### Information Collected Automatically
- Device type and iOS version
- App usage data (screens visited, features used)
- Crash logs and performance data

### Location Information
- If you grant permission, we may access your approximate location to suggest nearby destinations. You can revoke this permission at any time in your iOS Settings.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and improve the WanderBloom app experience
- Personalize travel recommendations
- Sync your data across devices (if applicable)
- Send optional notifications about saved trips or travel reminders
- Diagnose bugs and improve app performance

We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties.

---

## Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

---

## Third-Party Services

WanderBloom may use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:
- **Supabase** - database and authentication
- **Apple App Store** - in-app purchases and subscriptions

---

## Children's Privacy

WanderBloom is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
